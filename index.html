RAAGA

The Cultural Group Of Performing Art

We are extremely proud of our Cultural and ethnic background. Our primary goal is to preserve and nuture the cultural inheritance of Gujarat. Teaching, publishing and performing traditional folk dances at national as well as international stage are the indispensable essentials of Raaga. It contributes on cultural basis by participating in the various cultural programs organized by the Government of Gujarat. Raaga promotes the essence of the various folk dances in the interest of cultural inheritance of Gujarat like Raas, Garba, Sword Dance, Maniyaro, Rasdo, Tippani, Tribal Dances, Hudo etc.
